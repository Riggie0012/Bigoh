Guided Setup

Get your store ready in 15–30 minutes

Follow these steps once. You can always return here later.

1) Add your business details

Show customers you are real and verified.

  • Business name, address, and support hours.
  • Registration number and authority (optional).
  • Update in your .env file or admin settings.
2) Upload products

Add at least 5 products before going live.

  • Go to Upload Products.
  • Use clean, square product photos.
  • Write short, clear descriptions.
3) Set up Flash Sales

Boost sales with time‑limited offers.

  • Open Flash Sale.
  • Pick products and set a duration.
  • Turn the sale on when ready.
4) Choose Sponsored Products

Highlight your best items on the home page.

  • Go to Sponsored Products.
  • Select items you want to feature.
5) Check your store

Make sure everything looks good before sharing.

  • Visit the home page.
  • Open a product and test Add to Cart.
  • Confirm your contact info in the footer.
6) Go live

Share your store link and start selling.

  • Copy your site URL and share.
  • Respond fast to WhatsApp orders.
