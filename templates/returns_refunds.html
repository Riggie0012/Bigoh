Returns & Refunds Policy
Clear, fair, and designed to protect both customers and vendors.
Returns Window

Returns are accepted within 2-3 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging.

Please keep the original receipt and packaging to speed up verification.

Refunds

Refunds are processed after inspection. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Processing time depends on the payment provider and typically takes 3-7 business days.

Non-Returnable Items
  • Items that show signs of use or damage not caused by delivery.
  • Clearance items marked "Final Sale".
  • Personal or hygiene items that have been opened.
